# learning-contract.md

## 1. What I Expect From Myself
I expect to show up every day with focus and effort, even when it feels challenging or slow. I will dedicate time to practice, reflect on my progress, and push myself to try things I don’t fully understand yet. I will be honest about what I know, what I don’t know, and what I’m struggling with, so I can grow faster. I will take responsibility for my learning rather than waiting for someone to guide me every step.

---

## 2. What I Expect From This Bootcamp
I expect this bootcamp to provide structure, guidance, and real-world challenges that push me beyond my comfort zone. I expect clear feedback from instructors and peers, opportunities to build projects that matter, and daily drills that help me develop practical skills.

---

## 3. How I Will Work When I Feel Stuck
When I feel stuck, I will first pause and analyze what I have tried and where the problem lies. I will give myself time to debug independently, but if I cannot resolve it, I will reach out for help using my prepared daily updates or contacting Rahul or Shilpa. I will treat obstacles as part of the learning process, not as failures.

---

## 4. How I Will Measure My Progress
I will measure progress by tracking my daily coding practice, drills completed, and projects built. I will set weekly milestones for each tool (as the bootcamp focuses on one tool per week) and reflect on whether I achieved them.
